# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for all contributors.

Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in interactions.
- Avoid demeaning or exclusionary language and behavior.
- Address concerns by opening issues or contacting maintainers privately if appropriate.

Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers. Reports will be handled and investigated and may result in temporary or permanent bans.

For full, canonical text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/

Project maintainers may adapt the Contributor Covenant to fit project needs. If you need to report an incident, please open an issue titled "Code of Conduct report" or contact the maintainers directly.
